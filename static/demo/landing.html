Process

Three phases. Zero wasted motion.

01

Intake & Calibration

We spend 90 minutes understanding your career DNA — not just skills, but motivations, deal-breakers, and the role you actually want versus the one LinkedIn thinks you want.

02

Signal & Targeting

Our engine cross-references your profile against thousands of opportunities — including unlisted roles — and surfaces only the highest-fit matches. You review, we refine.

03

Outreach & Close

Warm intros, interview prep, offer negotiation. We stay in the loop from first ping to signed offer letter, optimizing at every stage.
